Biography


Where to begin? The beginning is always a good starting place. A long time ago, in a doctor's office in this very state, Saundra Karyn Clark, now AKA Kacy, was born. "How long ago?" you ask. See the next paragraph for a clue. This state is known as the bluegrass state, the Derby state - the great state of Kentucky. I was raised in Chicago.


I am a VietNam war veteran. I was enlisted and served with the Army Security Agency as a Chinese Mandarin linguist. I looked around and noticed the enlisted folks did all the work and made about a 1/4th of their supervisors' pay. I felt a change coming. I went back to college and became a Registered Nurse, giving up my dream to be a veterinarian. I wanted to go back into the military as an officer. I spent a total of 31 years on active duty and in reserves. I was a critical care nurse for 40 years.


I retired four years ago from nursing and have decided I need more income so I can pay off my house, have it demolished, and build a bunker into the hill where my house sits now. Voila`! I am now a fellow classmate of yours in this short and fast-paced course. Please feel free to help this Granny any time you are able. ;>)


On the lighter side, I have two sons, and five grand-children; 23 guineas, 6 roosters and 30 hens in the barn, 5 female cats and 14 kittens, 2 baby geese and 39 baby chickens in the house. Until just a few months ago, I also had 110 - yep, you read that right, 110 - chihuahuas (all in the house). I'm gearing up to buy big dogs (and probably a couple or three more tiny chis). I sure wanted to be a vet! Thanks for reading and hope you got a faint smile from reading this bio.
